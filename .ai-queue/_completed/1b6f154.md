## Instructions

### Signup vs Login differences

All signup blocks mirror the layout of their corresponding login variant (Signup01 ↔ Login01, etc.) but with these changes:

1. **Additional fields:** Name (first name or full name), email, password. Some variants may include confirm password.
2. **Footer link flipped:** "Already have an account? Sign in" (instead of "Don't have an account? Sign up")
3. **No forgot password link** — not relevant for signup
4. **Optional terms checkbox:** If `termsHref` or `privacyHref` props are passed, show a checkbox with "I agree to the [Terms of Service] and [Privacy Policy]"
5. **onSubmit data:** `{ name, email, password }` (instead of `{ email, password }`)
6. **Default titles/descriptions:** "Create an account" / "Enter your information to get started"

### Spec files

Each spec follows the block spec template. Key notes per variant:
- **Signup01:** Standard centered signup. Use for simple registration flows.
- **Signup02:** Two-column with image. Use for marketing-oriented signup pages.
- **Signup03:** Muted background. Use for standalone signup pages.
- **Signup04:** Image inside card. Use for compact signup with branding.
- **Signup05:** Social-first signup. Use when social auth is the primary registration path.
