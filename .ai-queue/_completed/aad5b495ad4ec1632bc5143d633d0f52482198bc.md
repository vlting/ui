### Sidebar15 — Left and Right Sidebars
A layout with sidebars on both sides. Left sidebar for primary navigation, right sidebar for contextual/secondary content.

**Props:** `Sidebar15Props` with:
- `leftGroups: NavGroup[]` — left sidebar navigation groups
- `rightGroups: NavGroup[]` — right sidebar navigation groups
- `leftHeader?: ReactNode`
- `rightHeader?: ReactNode`
- `leftFooter?: ReactNode`
- `rightFooter?: ReactNode`
- `children?: ReactNode` — main content area between sidebars
- `leftDefaultOpen?: boolean` (default: true)
- `rightDefaultOpen?: boolean` (default: true)
- `leftCollapsible?: 'offcanvas' | 'icon' | 'none'`
- `rightCollapsible?: 'offcanvas' | 'icon' | 'none'`
- `leftWidth?: number`
- `rightWidth?: number`

**Layout:**
- Container ViewJsx with flexDirection="row" and flex={1}
- Left Sidebar.Root (side="left") with leftGroups rendered via SidebarNavGroup
- Center content area (flex={1})
- Right Sidebar.Root (side="right") with rightGroups rendered via SidebarNavGroup
- Each sidebar independently collapsible

Create: `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar15.tsx`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar15.spec.md`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar15.test.tsx`

### Sidebar16 — Sidebar with Sticky Site Header
A standard sidebar combined with a sticky site header bar at the top. The header stays fixed while sidebar content scrolls.

**Props:** `Sidebar16Props extends SidebarBlockProps` plus:
- `siteTitle?: string`
- `siteLogo?: ReactNode`
- `headerActions?: ReactNode` (e.g., user avatar, settings button)

**Layout:**
- Outer container: ViewJsx with flex={1}
- Sticky header at top: styledHtml('header') with position="sticky", top=0, zIndex=10, flexDirection="row", alignItems="center", padding, borderBottom, background
  - Logo/title on left, actions on right
- Below header: ViewJsx flexDirection="row" with flex={1}
  - Sidebar.Root on left with standard group rendering
  - Main content area (children) on right with flex={1}

Create: `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar16.tsx`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar16.spec.md`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar16.test.tsx`
