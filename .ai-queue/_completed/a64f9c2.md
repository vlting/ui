## Completed Changes

- Select: `fontSize: 12` → `'$2'`
- Switch WIDTH_MAP: `sm: 48` → `'$4.5'`; THUMB_SIZE_MAP: `md: 20` → `'$1'`, `lg: 24` → `'$1.5'`
