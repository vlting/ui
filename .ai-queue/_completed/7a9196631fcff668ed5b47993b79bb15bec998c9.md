## Instructions

### Sidebar09 — Collapsible Nested Sidebars
Two-level sidebar: a primary "rail" sidebar on the left that collapses to icons (like Sidebar07), and when an item with children is selected, a secondary sidebar panel slides open next to it showing the children. Uses `collapsible="icon"` for the primary rail.

**Props:** `Sidebar09Props extends SidebarBlockProps`

**Layout:**
- Primary sidebar (narrow, icon-only when collapsed) shows top-level groups/items
- When an item with children is clicked, a secondary sidebar panel opens beside it
- The secondary panel shows the children of the selected item
- Use `useState` to track which top-level item is expanded
- The secondary panel uses another `Sidebar.Root` or a styled View panel

**Implementation approach:**
- Wrap both sidebars in a single container (ViewJsx with flexDirection="row")
- Primary sidebar: icon-only items (filter to items with icons, like Sidebar07)
- Secondary sidebar: shows children of the selected primary item
- When no item is selected, secondary panel is hidden

Create: `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar09.tsx`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar09.spec.md`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar09.test.tsx`

### Sidebar10 — Sidebar in a Popover
Instead of a persistent sidebar, navigation is accessed through a Popover triggered by a button. Good for space-constrained layouts.

**Props:** `Sidebar10Props extends Omit<SidebarBlockProps, 'open' | 'onOpenChange' | 'defaultOpen' | 'collapsible' | 'variant'>` plus:
- `triggerLabel?: string` (default: "Navigation")
- `placement?: 'bottom' | 'right'` (default: "bottom")

**Layout:**
- A trigger button that opens a Popover
- Inside the Popover.Content: a sidebar-style navigation panel with groups and items
- Does NOT use Sidebar.Root — instead composes a navigation panel manually using Popover.Root/Trigger/Content with groups rendered inside
- The navigation panel inside uses `<nav>` landmark (via styledHtml)

Create: `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar10.tsx`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar10.spec.md`, `packages/blocks/sidebar/Sidebar10.test.tsx`
