# Task: Replace pill-like radius on Spinner playground containers

## Context
`SpinnerSection.tsx` uses `radius: '$field'` on its container `StackY` elements, and `DarkStage` (shared.tsx) also defaults to `radius: '$field'`. In Popsicle theme, `$field` resolves to a large enough value that these containers look pill-shaped, which is undesirable for display containers.

## Requirements
- Replace `radius: '$field'` with `radius: '$3'` on the three `StackY` containers in `SpinnerSection.tsx` (lines 15, 26, 39)
- Update `DarkStage` in `shared.tsx` (line 108) from `radius: '$field'` to `radius: '$3'` — it's only used in SpinnerSection
- `$3` = `base * 0.75` = 3rem — noticeably less curved than `$field` (base = 4rem), and won't balloon in Popsicle theme
